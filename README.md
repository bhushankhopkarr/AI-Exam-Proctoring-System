# Proctoring-AI: Revolutionizing Exam Monitoring with Cutting-Edge Technology 🚀

Welcome to the future of exam proctoring – Proctoring-AI, an innovative project poised to transform the way exams are monitored using advanced artificial intelligence techniques. Let's take a journey through the groundbreaking functionalities of this state-of-the-art system:

## Vision-based Functionalities:
- **Eye Tracking:** Witness real-time eye tracking capabilities that monitor the candidate's gaze, ensuring focus and attention throughout the exam. 👀
- **Mouth Opening Detection:** Experience the power of AI as it detects any instances of mouth opening during the exam, ensuring integrity and authenticity. 😮
- **Person Counting and Mobile Phone Detection:** Marvel at the ability to count individuals and identify mobile phones in the exam environment, maintaining exam security and fairness. 📱
- **Head Pose Estimation:** Explore how AI accurately estimates head pose, providing insights into the candidate's focus and engagement. 💭
- **Face Spoofing Detection:** Delve into the realm of face spoofing detection, where AI distinguishes between real faces and fraudulent attempts, safeguarding the integrity of the exam process. 🔍

## Audio-based Functionalities:
- **Speech-to-Text Conversion:** Experience the seamless conversion of spoken words into text, enabling efficient monitoring and analysis of audio interactions during the exam. 🗣️
- **Stopword Removal and Comparison:** Witness how AI eliminates unnecessary noise by removing stopwords and compares exam content with question papers, ensuring compliance and integrity. 📝

**Important Note:**

Before diving into the world of Proctoring-AI, ensure you have the necessary prerequisites to embark on this technological journey:

1. **Python 3.8.10 Installation:** Make sure you have Python 3.8.10 installed on your system. You can download it from the official Python website or use a package manager like Anaconda.

2. **Virtual Environment Setup:** It's highly recommended to create a virtual environment for Proctoring-AI to manage dependencies effectively. You can create a virtual environment using tools like `venv` or `virtualenv`.

3. **Check 'models file.txt':** Don't forget to check the 'models file.txt' provided with the Proctoring-AI package. This file contains crucial information regarding the AI models used for various functionalities. Understanding these models is essential for effective utilization of Proctoring-AI.
